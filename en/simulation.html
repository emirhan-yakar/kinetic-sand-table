Interactive 3D

Pick a pattern, watch it draw

Live Three.js / WebGL simulation. Choose a pattern (signature, spiral…), the LED turns on and the ball carves it into the sand. Drag to rotate, scroll to zoom.

Tip: Drag to rotate (auto-rotate is off). You can explode the parts to see the inner mechanism. Keep hardware acceleration on if it feels slow.
