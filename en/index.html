How it works

Three parts, endless patterns

① Polar mechanism

A rotating arm (θ) with a sliding carriage (ρ). Every point is an (angle, radius) — sub-mm resolution.

② Magnet + ball

An N52 magnet on the carriage drags a steel ball beneath the sand; the ball carves a real groove.

③ Pattern + light

.thr patterns (signatures, spirals…) → G-code; a WS2812B LED ring gives a soft ambient glow.
